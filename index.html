Our In-Store Rarities

DAMN. (2017)

This modern Hip Hop classic, penned by Kendrick Lamar, released to massive critical acclaim. Come on down to check out this “Damn Red” vinyl, with an outer sleeve signed by “K.Dot” Lamar.

White Lighter (2013)

Released in 2013, Typhoon’s “White Lighter” was a seminal moment in Indie Rock history. Come into our store to see this Blood Red vinyl printing, signed by lead singer Kyle Morton himself.

Going Places!! (1965)

This 1960’s obscurity features the hit single “Spanish Flea”, one of the rare songs that everybody’s heard, but nobody seems to know the name of. Check out this wax rarity in store today!
